Teaching AI And Strategy

After three months it was finally time to say goodbye to my first cohort of students who bravely took the first (beta) edition of my new course “Strategy In The Age Of AI”. I wanted to teach this course for a long time as it combined my passion for strategy and organizations with my interest in algorithms, reinforcement learning, and adaptation. We started with the big picture, Industry 4.0, AI’s regulatory environment and non-market strategies, Blue Ocean Strategy for the AI era, the future of organizations and work, and bias and fairness. To complete the whole course, we finished with how to improve Human Intelligence (HI) a critical component to make a lot of AI work properly. As Pablo Picasso said, “Computers are useless.
